Write a query to find the name (first_name, last_name) of the employees who have a manager and worked in a USA based department. [2.5 marks] employees EMPLOYEE_ID FIRST_NAME LAST_NAME EMAIL PHONE_NUMBER HIRE_DATE JOB_ID SALARY COMMISSION_PCT MANAGER_ID DEPARTMENT_ID departments DEPARTMENT_ID DEPARTMENT_NAME MANAGER_ID LOCATION_ID locations location_id street_address postal_code city state_province country_id
Answere : SELECT first_name, last_name FROM employees where MANAGER_ID != null and DEPARTMENT_ID in (SELECT DEPARTMENT_ID from departments where locations = 'USA');


Consider above schemas and Write a query to find the name (first_name, last_name), and salary of the employees who earns more than the average salary and works in any of the IT departments. [2.5 marks]
answere
SELECT first_name, last_name FROM employees WHERE SALARY > (SELECT avg(SALARY) from employees) and DEPARTMENT_NAME = 'IT';
